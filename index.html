05 — Typography
TYPE
Display / Wordmark
Unbounded

Ultra-wide, ultra-heavy. No other restaurant in Uttam Nagar will touch this. Impossible to ignore on a signboard. Screams confidence.

Body / Menu / UI
Outfit Light

Geometric, warm, extremely legible at body sizes. Perfect contrast to the heavy display font. Ideal for menu item names, prices, and app UI.

08 — Why This Wins The Gig
01
Unbounded = Unforgettable
No restaurant in Uttam Nagar — probably in all of West Delhi — uses this typeface. It's impossible to confuse with anyone else. That's the point of a brand.
02
Two Colours, Infinite Flex
Yolk + Tangerine work together or separately. Hot signage, calm menus, loud packaging — one system covers every context without ever looking off-brand.
03
Built for the Digital World
The icon at 96px looks as sharp as at 16px. Zomato listing, Instagram grid, app icon, WhatsApp status — this identity was designed screen-first, print-second.
