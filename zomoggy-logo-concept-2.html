The flame mark is the icon — flame = heat, passion, cravings. It speaks food immediately without being literal (no pizza slice, no fork). Abstract enough to be premium. Recognizable enough to be remembered. Orange carries the existing brand color forward without any cost to reprinting.

Condensed, punchy, absolutely zero ambiguity. Used by Red Bull, Vice, Gorillaz. Makes the wordmark feel like a billboard headline — youth-coded but not childish.

07 — Why This Wins
01
The Z Pops
Making the Z in flame-orange creates an instant visual hook. Your eye goes there first, locks in the brand initial, then reads the full name. Pure memory engineering.
02
Flame = Food, Not Gaming
The icon is a flame — universally associated with cooking, heat, spice, desire. Replaces the lightning bolt with something that actually belongs in F&B.
03
Delhi Youth-Coded
Anton font is the same energy as streetwear drops and rap posters. Uttam Nagar youth crowd will feel this is theirs — while parents see it as premium.
